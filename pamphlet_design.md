### Brochure Mockup Breakdown:

#### 1. Overview Design
*   **Focus**: Value proposition, time-savings statistics, and high-level platform introduction.
*   **Colors**: Deep indigo and emerald green gradients matching your web styles.

#### 2. Detailed Features Design
*   **Focus**: Comprehensive list of the application's key technical features, with structured checkmarks and modern icons:
    *   **Room & Bed Manager**: Shared space log and occupancy rates.
    *   **Rent & Fee Automator**: Scheduled bills, invoicing logs, and checkout receipts.
    *   **Expense & Income Tracker**: Operational dashboards and charts.
    *   **Staff & Salary Log**: Employee directory and salary histories.
    *   **Visitor Security Logs**: Guest ledger.
    *   **Tenant Portal**: Support tickets, weekly menu board, and online payment proofs.
    *   **OTP Verification**: Strong security checkpoints.
    *   **Multi-Branch SaaS Onboarding**: Custom subdomain provisioning database setup.
